Welcome to the Ukulele section!

Ukulele is a fantastic instrument. Originating from Hawaii, the Ukulele has recently enjoyed quite a renaissance.

In many ways the ukulele behaves like a guitar and is a great starting point for little hands! Big hands are just as welcome though, just about anyone can learn the Ukulele, you just need a little time and a little dedication.

These four videos will guide you through your first notes to play a C major scale.

The Four Tellys are:

  1. Introduction Part 1: Open strings
  2. Introduction Part 2: Your first fretted note D or Re
  3. Introduction Part 3: Playing F or Fa
  4. Introduction Part 4: Playing Ti and another Do
