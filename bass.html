Where would we be without Bass guitar?

Reflect on that for a minute...

Bass is just as versatile an instrument as the guitar and it really brings home so many styles of music.

If you are an absolute beginner, why not have a work through some of them. The lessons are aimed at complete beginners so just bring your bass and thats it.

To start the lesson move over to the tellys on the right hand side and click them to watch the videos and listen to the tuning notes.

The two Tellys are:

  1. How to tune the Bass
  2. Play Along Open Strings

Have Fun!
