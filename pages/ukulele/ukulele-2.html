Introduction to Chords on Ukulele

Now that you have learnt your first scale it's time to play some chords

Chords in music are not trousers! They're a group of notes played together.

These five videos will teach you the chords: C, Am, F, G, G7.

All these chords are made from the scale of C Major from the previous lesson.

The Five Tellys are:

  1. Chords Part 0: Open Chord: C6/Am7
  2. Chords Part 1: C Major Chord
  3. Chords Part 2: Am Chord
  4. Chords Part 3: F Chord
  5. Chords Part 4: G and G7 Chord

Have Fun!
