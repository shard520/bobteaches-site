The C Major Scale on Ukulele

Now that you have tuned your instrument it's time to make some music.

Scales are the foundation of all music, some are more popular than others. The major scale is a great starting point.

These four videos will guide you through your first notes to play a C major scale.

The Four Tellys are:

  1. Introduction Part 1: Open strings
  2. Introduction Part 2: Your first fretted note D or Re
  3. Introduction Part 3: Playing F or Fa
  4. Introduction Part 4: Playing Ti and another Do
